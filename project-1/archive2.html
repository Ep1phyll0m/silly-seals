Artist Statement (Quynh's piece)

Below this statement is a list of contaminants that have been detected in wastewater processed within the relevant cities listed below. Water is an essential part of life, and studying its properties can give way to significant information regarding our health. In this case, the information shown below is a record of data recorded by WasterwaterScan, a comapny which seeks to spread information about the health of the people around us.
